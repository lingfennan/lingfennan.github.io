I am a Staff Security Researcher at Palo Alto Networks Inc. I received Doctoral degree from School of Computer Science at Georgia Tech, advised by Prof. Wenke Lee in IISP, co-advised by Prof. Brendan D. Saltaformaggio in CyFI. I received B.E. degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University. I used to work in Lab of Broadband Network Switching Technology and Communication advised by Prof. Bin Liu. More detail about me is available at [CV].

My current research interests are software security, web privacy and security, network security and mobile security. I have worked on several projects, such as software supply chain attack detection, automated open-source software risk management and simhash-based dynamic cloaking detection .
